# Kisan Direct

**Kisan Direct** is a web platform that connects farmers directly with customers, enabling fresh fruits and vegetables to be delivered straight from the farm. By removing intermediaries, the platform reduces unnecessary costs and helps farmers expand their business reach while offering customers fresher produce at fair prices.

---

## Overview

- Kisan Direct is a farm-to-home delivery platform that connects farmers directly to customers, delivering produce straight from the source.  
- By removing middlemen, the platform eliminates extra taxes and commissions that burden both customers and farmers.  
- Farmers save travel time and costs, reach a wider audience, and grow their business sustainably.  
- Customers receive fresher produce, better pricing, and a stronger connection to their food source.

## Why It Matters

Traditional agricultural supply chains involve multiple intermediaries, each adding cost and delay. **Kisan Direct** simplifies this by linking farmers and consumers directly.

- For farmers: lower operational costs, higher profit margins, and reduced risk.  
- For customers: better value, fresher produce, and greater trust in sourcing.  
- For the community: supports sustainable agriculture and reduces unnecessary logistics.

## How It Works (High-Level Flow)

1. **Farmer Registration:** Farmers sign up and list their available produce with details such as quantity, price, and origin.  
2. **Customer Browsing:** Customers explore the product catalogue and place orders directly through the platform.  
3. **Order Processing:** The system routes each order to the respective farmer or fulfillment partner.  
4. **Delivery:** Farmers or delivery partners dispatch produce directly to customers, minimizing logistics layers.  
5. **Payment and Feedback:** Farmers receive payment (minus minimal fees), and customers can provide feedback for continuous improvement.
